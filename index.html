About Us

We are an independent complete-service optometry practice dedicated to continually providing our clients with the highest standards of personalised care and service, combined with clinical excellence.

At CCO, we conduct thorough eye examinations using the latest generation, state-of-the-art equipment. Apart from testing your eyesight, we also examine for early signs of eye diseases such as cataracts, glaucoma and macular degeneration, all of which are leading causes of blindness in Australia.

Enjoy a truly unique optometry and shopping experience in a comfortable, stress-free ambience away from the traffic congestion, chaos and hassles of a shopping precinct.

Featured Products

MiSight™ contact lenses not only optically correct existing myopia, giving clear distance and near vision, but MiSight has also been proven to slow the progression of myopia in most children. Our Myopia Management System combines MiSight contact lenses with ActivControl™ Technology and strategies for better eye health.

MyoVision™ is a ZEISS single vision lens design with the innovative "Peripheral Vision Management" technology, enabling a reduction of myopia progression by an average of 30% in Asian children between 6 and 12 years with a history of parental myopia.

ORION™ Freeform technology uses state-of-the-art lens processing equipment and ORION’s advanced digital lens designs to deliver the most precisely crafted prescription possible. Customised ORION lenses are created to address our patients’ specific visual requirements, within 1/100 of a dioptre, resulting in easy adaptation and high patient satisfaction.
